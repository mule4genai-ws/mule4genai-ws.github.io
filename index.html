About the Workshop

Welcome to the MuleSoft for Salesforce Gen AI Workshop. This workshop aims to introduce you to the capabilities of MuleSoft in integrating and enhancing Agentforce, Salesforce's Gen AI functionalities. Learn how to harness the power of Agentforce, connect various data sources, and create intelligent workflows that enrich customer experiences.

The use case presented here is to set the business context for the workshop. The individual modules will dive deeper into the technical capabilities implementing the business use case.
